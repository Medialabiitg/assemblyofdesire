Assembly of Desire


Assembly of desire is a multidisciplinary symposium initiated by desire machine collective extending their project Periferry . The proposed programme will take place in at Majuli island in Assam during January 2018. The project employs contemporary art to stimulate and provoke discussion, and exchange ideas between different cultures and countries on the local and global issues defined by the two rivers. Contemporary art discourse frequently celebrates influences on artistic choice while rejecting attempts to delimit the semantic content of art. By contrast, this project posits the utility of contemporary art, not as a channel for didactic messages, but as a space for dialectical propositions. Such propositions aim to set up 'encounters.
